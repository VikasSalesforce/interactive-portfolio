‚öôÔ∏è Flow + Apex split
What I keep in Flow
  • Routing rules that admins may need to adjust themselves.
  • Notifications, simple updates, and record-triggered logic.
  • Screen flows for internal user workflows.
What I move to Apex
  • External API calls, complex transformations, and retries.
  • Operations sensitive to governor limits.
  • Any logic that must be versioned and unit tested like code.
